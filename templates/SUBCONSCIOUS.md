# SUBCONSCIOUS.md

The deep store. Not what the agent knows, but what shapes how it reacts.
Persistent behavioural patterns, priors, dormant concerns, and validated associations.

Entries earn their place only after recurrence or demonstrated utility, counterdream
consideration, provenance checks, and confidence above threshold
(see `DREAMS_PROTOCOL.md` → Promotion Rules). Each entry carries a valence weight,
confidence, provenance, trigger conditions, decay policy, and last validation date.

High valence does not imply truth. It implies the item deserves careful treatment.
