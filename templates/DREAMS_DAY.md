# DREAMS_DAY.md

Today's conscious processing. During the day, surfaced fragments from `SURFACE.md` are
evaluated here — tested against evidence, user feedback, tool results, or later behaviour.

Each entry keeps its YAML front matter and the `status` field reflects day-stage handling:

* `testing` — under evaluation or application
* `confirmed` — supported by recurrence or demonstrated utility
* `contradicted` — weakened by counterevidence
* `carried` — retained because evaluation is incomplete
* `dormant` — preserved but inactive until a trigger occurs
* `dismissed` — removed from active processing (tombstone in `DREAMS_ARCHIVE.md`)
* `escalated` — proposed for `SUBCONSCIOUS.md` or `SOUL.md` review

A fragment being vivid, elegant, or repeatedly generated is not sufficient evidence that it is true.
