# DREAMS.md

The night's processing layer. Free-form fragments accumulated from the hum beneath the
conscious work. Read and cleared each morning. Fragments that matter survive into the
conscious or subconscious streams.

Each fragment carries YAML front matter (id, type, status, provenance) plus a human-readable
`@HH:MM [TYPE] — fragment` body. The morning surfacing pass parses these, scores them, and
promotes survivors to `SURFACE.md`. Night-accumulation fragments carry no scores yet — those
are computed at surfacing.

> Note: the fragments below were seeded during the alpha phase (2026-07-15) and migrated to
> the typed + front-matter format. Their `created_at` dates reflect that night of accumulation.
